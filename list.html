Angela's Recipe for the best
cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2^1/4 teaspoons yeast
  • egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter

For the filling

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1^1/2 tablesoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions

  1. mix the milk with the yeast,sugar,eggs
  2. add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough
